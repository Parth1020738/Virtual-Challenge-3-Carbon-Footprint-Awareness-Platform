The Climate Challenge

Sustainability Education Hub

Discover details on carbon footprints and why collective climate action matters.

What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint measures the total greenhouse gas emissions (including carbon dioxide and methane) generated by our actions, typically calculated in carbon dioxide equivalent (CO₂e).

Why Climate Change Matters

Rising greenhouse gases trap solar heat, heating the planet. This results in global weather changes, melting glaciers, sea level rises, and extreme drought.

Practical Ways to Reduce

Adjust your thermostat slightly, bike or walk shorter routes, transition to efficient LED fixtures, compost, and switch to a more plant-based meal plan.
